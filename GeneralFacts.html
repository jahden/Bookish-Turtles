General Facts

Like the mud turtles, musk turtles will live to be around 50 years old.
They grow to about 3 to 4 inches in length and stay that size forever.
Musk turtles make great pets and are good for owners new to raising turtles.
The Stinkpot Common Musk Turtle is actually one of the smallest turtles in the world.
